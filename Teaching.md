# Teaching Experience

I previously held the position of Associate Lecturer at the Research School of Economics at the Australian National University (ANU). I have taught various causes related to macroeconomics and public economics with SELT (student evaluation) scores consistently above 4.5.

I have also worked as a teaching assistant in economics for the College of Business and Economics (CBE) and the College of Arts and Social Sciences (CASS) at the ANU.

- ECON3023/ECON4403/ECON8003 Economic Policy Issues

- EMET7001 Mathematical Techniques for Economic Analysis
